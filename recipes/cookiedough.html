Edible Cookie Dough

Description

This delicious edible cookie dough is easy to make and safe to eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ cup milk chocolate chips
  • ½ cup mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. To heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use: Place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring it every 15 seconds. Set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large bowl until creamy.
  3. Beat in vanilla extract and salt.
  4. Add heat-treated flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  5. Stir in milk until dough is just combined.
  6. Fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips.
