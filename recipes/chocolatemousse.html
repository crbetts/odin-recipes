Chocolate Mousse

A smooth and easy to make chocolate mousse. Perfect for a summer night.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a large bowl and beat with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  2. Pipe mousse into 6 shot glasses. Serve immediately or chill until serving.
